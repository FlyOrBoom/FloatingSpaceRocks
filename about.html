Space Rocks is a second‑year robotics team based in Southern California.

Created in August 2018 as part of Major STEM Academy, our team consists of a group of students who are not only passionate towards science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, but also eager to develop skills in communication, collaboration, teamwork, and leadership.

We are proudly sponsored by Collins Aerospace.
